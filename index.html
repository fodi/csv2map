This webapp turns rows of CSV files into markers on an interactive map. It's just some glue between PapaParse and Leaflet, powered by OpenStreetMap.

To get started, just enter the URL of your CSV file (eg. a Google Sheet published in that format will turn into this).

The file must include a Latitude and Longitude column. Optional columns are PopupHTML and MarkerColor.
